Micro-Publication

Brief

For a week we were given the project of designing our own micro-publication. I came up with the idea of a fluffy monster which would be a classroom friend for children.

Mood

It would be in the shape of a cube and depending on the childâ€™s mood the fluffy monster would change colour and facial expresson. It is primarily aimed at shy children and encourages them to speak to their teacher about their worries and fears. In addition, all conversations between the monster and the child are recorded so the teacher can listen to them via an app.

I started off this project with mind maps to try and generate inspiration. I then did some basic sketches to get what I wanted it to look like from my head onto paper so I could visualise it better.

I found it difficult for this project deciding what type of product I wanted to design, who I wanted to aim it at and what purpose it would have. Now with my final design I feel that it is child friendly and a practical idea that children would benefit from to stop bullying and make their classroom experience better.
