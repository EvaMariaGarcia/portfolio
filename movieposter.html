Movie Posters

Brief

At the start of my first year we were introduced to the fundamentals of the craft of communication design, both off- and on-screen. To put our knowledge to the test we were given a small project to design a few movie posters with a Josef Müller-Brockmann style to them.

From doing research on Josef Müller-Brockmann I wanted to keep my movie posters minimal. I started off on paper by doing mind maps to get an idea of what movies I wanted to choose and then what things about the movie I could take to include in the poster. I decided to do my movies on Monster Inc. and Coraline.

I had a few set backs with this project because at the time I was only really getting familiar with using illustrator and how to use pathfinder etc. I also didn’t really know who Josef Müller-Brockmann was so I had to do a fair bit of research on him to draw inspiration on his style.
